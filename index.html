// FAQ

Questions we get asked
before every engagement.

If you have one we haven't answered here, just ask.

How quickly can you actually deploy something?
Our fastest deployment was 48 hours from kickoff call to production. Most single-workflow automations go live in 5–7 business days. More complex multi-agent systems typically take 2–3 weeks depending on data access and integrations.
Do we own the code and systems you build?
Yes, completely. You get full source code, full documentation, and systems deployed on your own infrastructure. There is no vendor lock-in with Operon — if we stopped working together tomorrow, your systems keep running and you can maintain them independently.
What do you need from us to get started?
Very little upfront. A 30-minute audit call, access to 1–2 people who know the workflow you want to automate, and read access to the relevant data sources. We handle the rest. We're designed to work with busy teams, not add to your workload.
How is this different from using off-the-shelf AI tools?
Off-the-shelf tools are built for generic use cases. We build systems around your specific data, your logic, your workflows, and your definitions of quality. We also ship with eval harnesses that measure whether the system is actually working — something no SaaS tool gives you out of the box.
What happens if the AI makes a mistake?
Every system we build includes automated quality scoring (LLM-as-judge), human-in-the-loop checkpoints where appropriate, monitoring, and alerting. We design for graceful failure — the system flags uncertainty rather than guessing. We also establish baseline accuracy benchmarks before go-live.
Do you work with non-technical teams?
Absolutely. We handle all the technical implementation. Your team interacts with the outputs — dashboards, Slack alerts, automated reports — not the code. We document everything clearly and train whoever needs to oversee the system after handoff.
